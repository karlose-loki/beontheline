FAQ

Answers to common questions about our fashion collective.

What is the NZ/CA Fashion Collective?

We are a digital platform and community dedicated to celebrating and exploring the unique fashion cultures of New Zealand and Canada.

How do you choose which designers to feature?

Our curation team focuses on brands and designers that show a strong connection to their local culture, a commitment to quality, and a unique creative vision.

Can I contribute an article or suggest a brand?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. Please use the contact form on our home page to send us your ideas, suggestions, or pitches.

Do you sell clothing?

Currently, we are a content and discovery platform, not a retailer. We provide inspiration and information, often with links to where you can purchase items directly from the brands.

Is your content free to access?

Yes, all our articles, style guides, and features are completely free to access. We are committed to making fashion inspiration accessible to everyone.

How can I get involved with the community?

Start by following us on social media and signing up for our newsletter! We're also planning to launch a community forum soon for more direct interaction.

Who is behind this project?

We are a passionate group of writers, stylists, and creatives from both New Zealand and Canada who believe our local fashion scenes deserve a global spotlight.

Is an account required?

No account is needed to browse our content. We prioritize instant access so you can start exploring right away.
