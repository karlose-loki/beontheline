Wellington's Vintage Scene: A Treasure Hunter's Guide

Where to find the best pre-loved and retro gems in New Zealand's capital.

Published 2025-08-22

New Zealand's capital is a goldmine for vintage lovers. We share our favorite spots for finding unique pieces with a story to tell.

Cuba Street Finds

The heart of Wellington's bohemian culture, Cuba Street is lined with vintage boutiques and op-shops where you can find everything from 70s denim to classic NZ-made wool coats.

Recycled Boutiques

Discover high-end consignment stores offering curated collections of designer labels and quality garments. It's sustainable shopping at its most stylish.

Market Treasures

Don't miss the weekend markets, where local collectors and fashion enthusiasts sell unique, pre-loved items. It's the perfect place to find a one-of-a-kind souvenir.
