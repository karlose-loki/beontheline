How to Explore

The goal is simple: discover styles you love and learn more about their origins.

  • Interact: Use your Mouse or simply Tap on items in the lookbook to learn more about them.
  • Discover: Each item reveals details about the designer, materials, and cultural context. Collect your favorites!
  • Controls: Press P to pause the experience and R to save your curated board and start fresh.
